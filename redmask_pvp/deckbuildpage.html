Deckbuilding

Building a deck for PvP is the most crucial thing a wizard can do. When you're starting out it can be pretty daunting and difficult to build a deck, but here are some basic guidelines: In your main deck try putting cards that have a gambit based on your strengths, cards that counter any type of enemies strengths (mostly focusing on shields and weaknesses), and any debuffing cards that may help you. In the side deck make sure to have plenty of tower shields and weaknesses, and have a few spirit and elemental shields, and lastly a couple bigger hitting cards for those emergency hits.

The main strategy you should focus on at this lower level PvP is to get a Gambit card in hand after setting up the gambit.
